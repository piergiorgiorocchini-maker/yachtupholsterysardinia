Last updated: 29 June 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website function correctly, remember preferences, measure traffic or support advertising activity.

2. Cookies used by this website

This website may use technical cookies necessary for basic site operation. It is also prepared to use analytics and advertising technologies, depending on the tracking tools enabled and your consent choices.

3. Analytics and advertising tools

The website tracking system is designed to support Google Analytics 4, Google Ads conversion tracking and Meta Pixel. These tools may collect information about page views, clicks, WhatsApp interactions, phone clicks, email clicks and form submissions where configured and permitted.

4. Consent

Where consent is required, analytics and advertising storage are denied by default until consent is granted. You can manage cookie permissions through the consent banner or browser settings when available.

5. Third-party cookies

Third-party services such as Google, Meta, WhatsApp or email providers may process data according to their own policies when you interact with their services.

6. Managing cookies

You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect tracking, measurement or certain website features, because apparently websites still need crumbs to remember things.
