Focus on the upholstery guests see first

The priority areas are usually salon sofas, guest seating, cushions, fabric panels and soft surfaces near high-use areas. These surfaces collect dust, body oils, sunscreen traces, humidity-related odours and small marks over time.

A focused assessment helps decide whether a full upholstery cleaning service is needed or whether specific areas should be prioritised first.

Yacht schedules in Porto Cervo can change quickly. A photo-based WhatsApp assessment helps avoid wasting time before anyone commits to a visit.
