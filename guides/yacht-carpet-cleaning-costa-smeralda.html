Local guide

Yacht carpets carry the traffic of the whole season.

In Costa Smeralda, yacht carpets take the abuse quietly: guests, crew, luggage, salt air, sunscreen, food traces and everyday movement. Then everyone acts shocked when marks appear. Charming species, humans.

A focused yacht carpet cleaning service helps restore a fresher, cleaner and more consistent presentation before charter, owner use or premium guest visits.

The practical first step is simple: send photos by WhatsApp with the yacht location, timing and visible textile issue. Mr. Rocchini personally reviews the request before suggesting the next step.

Start with high-traffic carpet areas

The most important carpet areas are usually the salon, cabin entrances, corridors, stair transitions and guest walkways. These zones tend to show marks and general dullness before lower-use areas.

A photo assessment can quickly identify whether the carpet issue is localised or whether a broader refresh is more sensible before guest use.

Cabin carpets need separate attention

Cabin carpet issues are often less visible in wide photos but more noticeable to guests once they use the space. Marks, odours and tired textile areas can reduce the perceived freshness of the cabin.

For a useful assessment, send both wide cabin photos and close-ups of any specific carpet issue.

Timing matters in Costa Smeralda

During the high season, yacht schedules move quickly. Carpet cleaning should be assessed before the last possible moment, especially when guest boarding, charter preparation or owner arrival is involved.
